About The Foundation

Building churches, empowering communities, and creating sustainable kingdom impact.

Who We Are

Mount Zion Game Changers Foundation is a faith-driven initiative focused on advancing church infrastructure, education, and community empowerment through structured, transparent, and impactful development projects.

Our Mission

To transform church development and community empowerment through accountability, structured funding, and sustainable kingdom-driven projects.

Key Outcomes from Foundational Meeting

Structured Funding

Proposed elimination of harambees in favor of structured contributions and accountability systems.

Transparency & Governance

Bank account setup, official signatories, audits, and constitution development.

Sustainable Growth

Funding church projects, schools, hospitals, and long-term kingdom investments.

Esther Wanjeri – Chairperson

Visionary Leader & Founder

Esther Wanjeri is the driving force behind the Mount Zion Game Changers Foundation. She initiated the vision in December 2024 with a mission to bring structure, accountability, and sustainability to church development projects. She leads strategic planning, coordination, and mobilization of members, ensuring that every initiative aligns with the kingdom agenda of empowering communities and advancing the church. Her leadership emphasizes transparency, impact, and long-term transformation.

Wesly Langat – Vice Chairperson

Spiritual & Strategic Leader

Wesly Langat serves as the Vice Chairperson of the Mount Zion Game Changers Foundation, providing both spiritual guidance and strategic direction. He plays a key role in aligning the foundation’s mission with biblical principles and ensuring that all initiatives advance the church’s long-term vision. With a deep commitment to kingdom work, Wesly supports leadership coordination, decision-making processes, and mentorship, helping to drive impactful and sustainable development across church communities.

Bilha Mihindo – Treasurer

Financial Steward & Accountability Leader

Bilha Mihindo serves as the Treasurer of the Mount Zion Game Changers Foundation, overseeing financial management, accountability, and transparency. She is an avid businesswoman instrumental in ensuring that all funds are properly managed, audited, and aligned with the foundation’s mission of structured and sustainable development. With a strong commitment to integrity and stewardship, Bilha plays a critical role in building trust among members and ensuring that every contribution delivers meaningful impact.

Gabriella Wekesa – Secretary

Software Engineer & Administrative Coordinator

Gabriella Wekesa serves as the Secretary of the Mount Zion Game Changers Foundation, where she oversees documentation, records management, and official communication. As a Software Engineer, she brings a strong background in systems development, organization, and digital solutions, helping to streamline processes and improve operational efficiency within the foundation. Gabriella plays a key role in ensuring that all meetings, decisions, and activities are well-documented and aligned with the foundation’s goals. Her attention to detail and structured approach contribute to transparency, accountability, and effective coordination across all initiatives.
