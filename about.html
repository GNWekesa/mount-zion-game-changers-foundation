About The Foundation

Building churches, empowering communities, and creating sustainable kingdom impact.

Who We Are

Mount Zion Game Changers Foundation is a faith-driven initiative focused on advancing church infrastructure, education, and community empowerment through structured, transparent, and impactful development projects.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a self-sustaining organization that transforms the infrastructural landscape of the Mount Zion Seventh Day Church of God in Kenya and beyond through strategic resource pooling, administration, transparency, and impact-driven action.

Mission

To improve the physical infrastructure of the Mount Zion Seventh Day Church of God in Kenya and beyond by pooling resources from members and well-wishers to support church building projects.

Key Outcomes from Foundational Meeting

Structured Funding

Proposed elimination of harambees in favor of structured contributions, accountability, and sustainable financial systems.

Transparency & Governance

Official bank account setup, signatories, audits, constitution development, and operational accountability.

Sustainable Growth

Supporting long-term kingdom investments including churches, schools, hospitals, and community transformation initiatives.
